About Our School

Committed to Excellence in Education Since Our Foundation

Our History

Kanti Secondary School is one of the respected educational institutions in Butwal, dedicated to providing quality education, discipline, and character development.

Since its establishment, the school has helped thousands of students achieve academic success while encouraging leadership, creativity, sportsmanship, and social responsibility.

Mission • Vision • Values

Mission

To provide quality education that develops responsible, confident and skilled citizens.

Vision

To become a leading educational institution producing capable leaders for Nepal and beyond.

Core Values

Discipline, Respect, Honesty, Innovation, Excellence and Teamwork.
